Additional income

You can tell us about your additional income or expenses using your online child maintenance account. We will tell you how to set this up later in the application.

You’ll need to provide evidence of your additional income or expenses, and they may change the amount of child maintenance you pay.

Additional income can include:

  • receiving over £2500 a year in rent from property or land
  • receiving over £2500 a year in dividends or interest from savings or investments
  • earning an income of over £100 a week from employment or self-employment in addition to any benefits you get
  • some of your income is paid to another person through a limited company
  • increased pension contributions
  • employment benefits that reduce your take-home salary. For example, a company car
  • assets valued over £31,250. For example, cash, property, land, gold, stocks, shares, unit trusts, gilt-edged securities, or crypto currency
