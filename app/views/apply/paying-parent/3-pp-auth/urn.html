What is your unique reference number?

You may have received an email or text message with your unique reference number. It starts with 85, 95 or 96.

If you do not have a unique reference number, you can apply for one through Get Help Arranging Child Maintenance.

I need to pay child maintenance to more than one parent

You need to make a separate application for each parent. Child Maintenance Options should have emailed you different unique reference numbers.

Call Child Maintenance Options if you need a new unique reference number.
