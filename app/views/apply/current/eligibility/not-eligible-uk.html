You need to call

You’ll need to apply by phone if you or the other parent live outside the UK and work for:

  • the Civil Service
  • Her Majesty’s Diplomatic Service
  • the armed forces
  • a company that is based and registered in the UK
  • the NHS
  • a UK local authority

If you or the other parent do not work for any of the organisations listed above, you can call the Child Maintenance Service to get advice on your options.

Child Maintenance Service (England, Scotland and Wales)
Telephone: 0800 171 2345
Relay UK: 18001 0800 171 2345

Monday to Friday, 9.30am to 3.30pm (excluding bank holidays)
Calls to the Child Maintenance Service are free from landlines and mobiles

Child Maintenance Service (Northern Ireland)
Telephone: 0800 171 2345
Relay UK: 18001 0800 232 1956

Monday to Friday, 9.30am to 3.30pm (excluding bank holidays)
Calls to the Child Maintenance Service are free from landlines and mobiles
